Lemon Pepper Chicken Wings

Description

Version of Wingstop's lemon-pepper chicken wings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups oil
  • 2tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1tbsp lemon-pepper seasoning
  • 12 chicken wings

Directions

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Heat oil in deep-fryer to 375deg F
  3. Stir olive oil and lemon-pepper seasoning in a bowl
  4. Fry wings in hot oil until no longer pink at the bone and the juices run clear, about 8 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted near the bone should read 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
  5. Toss hot wings with lemon-pepepr mixture to coat
